VARIETIES

Alphonso

Often considered the "king of mangoes," Alphonso is prized for its rich, sweet flavor, smooth texture and saffron-colored flesh.

Banganapalli

Banganapalli mangoes are large-sized with an oblong shape and yellow skin. They have a sweet taste and are primarily cultivated in Andhra Pradesh.

Kesar

Known for its intense aroma and sweet taste, Kesar mangoes have a distinct orange-yellow flesh. They are primarily cultivated in the Gujarat region.

Dasheri

Dasheri mangoes are renowned for their sweet, juicy flavor and fiberless flesh. They are elongated in shape with a greenish-yellow skin and are primarily grown in northern India, particularly in Uttar Pradesh.

Langra

Langra mangoes are known for their unique flavor, which is a perfect blend of sweetness and tartness. They are primarily cultivated in Bihar.

Malgova

Malgova mangoes, also known as Malgoa or Malgoba, are another highly esteemed mango variety, particularly in South India. These mangoes are renowned for their large size, distinct shape, and delicious flavor. The peak season for Malgova mangoes typically spans from April to June.
